Before reporting a tuner problem

  • Allow microphone access when prompted.
  • Move away from other instruments, speakers, and background noise.
  • Pluck one open string and let it ring.
  • Stop microphone listening before playing a reference tone.

Useful details to include

Please include your device model, iOS version, Banjo Lab version, and the steps that reproduce the problem. Do not post personal or sensitive information.
